We provide a general solution in most instances first.\\

$\begin{cases}\displaystyle
	\frac{v_I-v_-}{R_1}=\frac{v_--v_O}{R_2}=i_2\\
	v_+=v_-=0\\
	i_O+i_2=i_L\\
	i_L=\displaystyle\frac{v_O}{R_L}

\begin{cases}\displaystyle
	v_O=-\frac{R_2}{R_1}v_I\\
	i_2=\displaystyle\frac{v_I}{R_1}\\

	i_L=\displaystyle\frac{v_O}{R_L}
\end{cases}$\\
